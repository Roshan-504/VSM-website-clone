Transformation Through Mentoring

What We Do

Vidyadaan Sahayyak Mandal, Thane (VSM Thane) was founded in 2008 when a few like-minded people got together to help deserving students from Maharashtra’s rural areas who couldn’t pursue higher education due to weak financial position. Very early in its journey, VSM realised that it was the quality of help that mattered more than the quantity.

That has been the abiding mantra of VSM ever since. VSM developed the unique mentor-student model to transform the students into confident, responsible, and independent members of the society

VSM ADMISSIONS FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2024-25 STARTED

VSM's journey that started with one student has increased many fold over the years. VSM admission process has enabled us to reach needy and deserving candidates across the state with deep penetration into rural Maharashtra. With each passing year and increasing number of students approaching us, we are committed to make this process more robust and sustainable. Please click below to know more about the VSM Admission process.

ACHARYA ATRE KATTA, THANE FELICITATES VSM WITH ITS KRUTADNYATA PURASKAR

Acharya Atre Katta, Thane felicitates VSM with Krutadnyata Puraskar on May 4, 2024. As of now over 1300 personalities have graced the Katta to spread awareness about their field of work. Krutadnyata Puraskar is awareded to the personalities or organization who have been the torch bearers of making a change in the social fabric of our nation. Ms. Geeta Tai Shah humbly accepted the award and assured VSM's continued commitment in the field of education.

VSM STUDENTS PARTICIPATING IN SOCIAL CAMPS TO INCULCATE SOCIAL SENSITIVITY

In the month of May, VSM students will be participating in annual ‘Shramsanskar Chavni’, a 10 day long event at Somnath organized by Maharogi Seva Samiti that aims to inculcate and instill important values like work ethics, punctuality, time management, teamwork etc. in the campers. Throughout the year, VSM students participate in various other social camps too like, One Week for Nation, Sane Guruji Smarak Shibit to grow as a socially sensitive member of the society.
